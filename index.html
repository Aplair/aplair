Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED
QUESTIONS.

What is professional real estate video editing?
Our expert real estate video editing (or montage) is a strategic process of cutting property footage to maximize viewer retention, boost perceived value, and trigger fast inquiries.
Do you edit drone reels for property tours?
Yes, we specialize in drone reel montage, adding property boundary tracking and cinematic effects to make your listings stand out on social media.
How long does video delivery take?
Our standard delivery time is 2 to 5 business days. However, it may take slightly longer depending on the project complexity and the chosen package. We always set clear deadlines in advance so you can launch your campaign on time.
What if I need revisions?
Your satisfaction is our priority. The basic package includes 2 revisions, while the Premium real estate Bundle package comes with 5 revisions to ensure you are completely satisfied with the result. We want your property to look its absolute best.
Do you provide AI voiceover in Arabic?
Yes, absolutely! We provide realistic and emotional AI voiceovers in both English and various Arabic dialects (Gulf, Egyptian, Modern Standard).
How do I send you the raw video clips?
Once the project details are agreed upon, we will provide you with a secure cloud folder link (such as Google Drive or Dropbox) where you can easily upload raw clips, drone footage, and brand elements like your logo.
